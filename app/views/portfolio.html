Zasca

2012-2013

Social Content Curator, Content Sharing Tool and Gamification Marketing Portal. Allowed users to browse a huge number of post by topic, order by relevancy, post new content, and participate in tournaments to win prizes.
Zasca is my biggest achievement so far, probably because I was the only developer assigned to the project and programmed the whole site on my own. I had to play all the technical roles associated to the development of a web app: programmer, analyst, DBA, UX developer, CTO... As such, I had freedom to decide on technologies, succesfully choosing MongoDB, Redis, Node.js, AMD with Require.js, Grunt.js, ElasticSearch, AngularJS, Twitter and Facebook API. This is also the project where I started to embrace TDD.
